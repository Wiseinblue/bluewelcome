    📖 How to use BlueWelcome

    Welcome! This editor is where you build the digital guide your guests will see. It takes about 15 minutes the first time. Fill in the sections on the left, then press Save guide. You can come back and change anything, anytime.

    💡 Golden rule: after making changes, always press Save guide (top right). Nothing is saved until you do.

    🚀 Quick start (the 5 essentials)

    If you only have a few minutes, fill in these and you already have a working guide:

    1. Property → house name, city, and at least one photo.
    2. Wi-Fi → network name and password.
    3. Check-in → arrival time and how to get in.
    4. Contacts → at least your phone number.
    5. Press Save guide. Done — your guide is live!

    📋 Every section explained

    🏠 Property

    The basics guests see first. Name and City are required (marked with *). Add a friendly Welcome message, pick your accent colour, and upload your photos and (optional) house logo.

    Photos: the first photo is the cover (shown big at the top) — it has a "Cover" badge. Use the small ‹ › arrows under each photo to reorder them, and the ✕ to delete. Upload as many as you like.

    📄 The house optional

    Tell the story of the house, the hosts, the area. Don't need it? Switch off Visible and the tab disappears for guests.

    📶 Wi-Fi

    Network name and password. A QR code is created automatically — guests scan it and connect with one tap, no typing.

    🔑 Check-in & 🚪 Check-out

    The arrival/departure time and clear step-by-step instructions (keys, door code, parking, rubbish, windows). Add one step at a time with the "+ Add" button — they show as a numbered list to guests.

    📋 House rules optional

    The rules guests should follow (no smoking, quiet hours, no pets…). Add one rule at a time.

    📞 Contacts

    Add at least one (usually you, the owner). For each contact: name, phone, and whether it's on WhatsApp. You can mark a contact as Emergency or Medical — these get grouped and highlighted for guests.

    📍 Nearby (Explore)

    Everything around the house:

    • How to find us: address, a Google Maps link, written directions, and photos of the gate/entrance/parking so guests recognise the place. (Tip: for the map, just paste the Google Maps link — if you paste the whole "embed" code, we'll figure it out anyway.)
    • Area guide: if you already have a guide of the area (a website or PDF), link it here and guests get a "Discover the area" button.
    • Restaurants, Attractions, Transport: your local recommendations.

    Each of these has a Visible switch — turn off what you don't want to show. Your data stays saved, just hidden.

    ❓ FAQ optional

    Common questions and answers (air conditioning, where to put rubbish, early check-in…). Guests tap a question to open the answer.

    ⚙️ Settings

    Here you can: change your guide's web link name (the word after the address, e.g. .../villarosa), change your password, and log out.

    📸 Adding photos (works everywhere)

    Anywhere you see Upload, you can pick a photo from your phone or computer, or drag it in. Photos are shrunk automatically so the guide stays fast — you don't need to resize anything. You can also paste a web link to an image.

    👁️ Preview & 🔗 sharing with guests

    • Press Preview (top) to see the guide exactly as your guests will, before saving.
    • Your guide has its own web link (you set the name in Settings). Share that link with guests, or turn it into a QR code to print and leave in the house.
    • Guests don't need to log in or install anything — they just open the link.

    🌍 Languages

    The guide is in English. Guests who speak another language can use their browser's built-in "Translate page" (Chrome, Safari, Edge all have it) to read it in their own language automatically — you don't need to do anything.

    🆘 Troubleshooting

    • "I saved but don't see the change": on your phone, fully close the guide tab and reopen it, or pull down to refresh. Changes are saved — it's just the old page in memory.
    • "A tab is missing for guests": check its Visible switch is on, and that you filled in at least some content for it.
    • "I forgot my password": contact Wise in Blue — we'll reset it for you.
    ✅ Remember: press Save guide after every change. That's the one thing you must not forget!
